About Merge

Emerging from the depths of the underground techno scene, Merge Konflict is the sonic alias of a producer obsessed with the tension between mechanical precision and human emotion. Characterized by relentless low-end frequencies, industrial textures, and hypnotic rhythms, the Merge Konflict sound is designed for expansive warehouses and intimate dark rooms alike.

With releases on seminal labels and a touring schedule that spans the globe, the project continues to evolve, pushing the boundaries of contemporary electronic music while remaining fiercely loyal to its raw, unpolished roots.
